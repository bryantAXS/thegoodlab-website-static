Winning For Winston

Winning for Winston is an organization that provides support, education and fundraising for ALS research. Created by Michael Winston, who was diagnosed with ALS in 2008, the organization holds various fundraisers and auctions throughout the year. We were happy to help with the design and development of small micro-site promoting one of their live auctions. The site was built with mobile in mind and was optimized for viewing on both the iPhone and the iPad.

Ringler Associaes

The project, which was through One Design Company, required a full site build, with several custom components such as a geo-location based search, displaying of client stories, and consultant contact and referral forms.
