The Post Family

The Post Family is a group of 7 Chicago-based artists and designers who stand at the forefront of the Chicago art scene. They are a gallery, letterpress and screen printing studio, experimental music venue and creative incubator, whose works span across all mediums and types of art. Working closely with The Post Family we were able to create an engaging experience that brought the collective mind of each family-member to life via their interactive blog and website.
